The island that starts everything

Koh Samui isn't just where Ocean United is based — it's a destination in its own right. Before your catamaran ever clears the harbor, there's a coastline here worth seeing slowly: limestone headlands, quiet bays tucked behind the resort strip, and a horizon that's usually doing something interesting at sunrise.

Most charters use Koh Samui as the launch point for trips further out, but plenty of guests build a route that hugs the island itself — working along the southern coves where the crowds thin out fast, or anchoring offshore for an afternoon swim before heading back in. If your trip includes any time onboard before or after a longer route, this is where it happens.

On the water

Because Koh Samui routes stay close to shore, this is the lower-key option in the fleet's lineup — better suited to a relaxed afternoon than a full adventure day. It also pairs well as a warm-up or wind-down leg on trips headed to or from Koh Phangan, Koh Tao, or Koh Nang Yuan.
