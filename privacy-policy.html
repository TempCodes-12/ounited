1. Information We Collect

When you inquire about or book a charter with Ocean United, we may collect information you provide directly, including your name, email address, phone number, preferred travel dates, group size, and any details you share about your trip.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to respond to inquiries, confirm bookings, coordinate trip logistics with our crew, and communicate with you about your charter before and after your trip. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

3. Information Sharing

We may share limited booking details with our onboard crew and operational partners solely for the purpose of fulfilling your charter. We do not share your information with third parties for marketing purposes without your consent.

4. Data Retention

We retain booking and contact information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, including ongoing customer service and legal recordkeeping requirements.

5. Your Choices

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of the personal information we hold about you by contacting us directly using the details on our Contact page.

6. Cookies & Website Data

Our website may use basic cookies or analytics tools to understand how visitors use the site. This data is used to improve the website experience and is not used to identify individual visitors.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your information is handled, please reach out via our Contact page.
