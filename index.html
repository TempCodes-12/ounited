About Ocean United

Built by people who'd rather be on the water than talking about it.

Ocean United started with a simple idea: chartering a boat in Koh Samui shouldn't mean choosing between a tired old longtail and a brochure that overpromises. We run a small, well-kept fleet of three catamarans, and we run it the way we'd want it run for ourselves.

No mass-market tour groups, no rushed itineraries. Every charter is built around where you want to go, how long you want to stay, and how many people are coming with you. Koh Tao for the reefs, Koh Phangan for the coastline, Koh Nang Yuan for the sandbar everyone's seen in photos and nobody believes is real until they're standing on it.
